remix
=====
David Hajage <dhajage[at]gmail[dot]com>

== Without +ascii+ package

<<>>=
library(remix)
remix(data = esoph)
remix(cbind(...) ~ tobgp, esoph)
remix(cbind(...) ~ Species, iris)
@

== With +ascii+ package

<<results = ascii>>=
library(remix)
remix(data = esoph)
remix(cbind(...) ~ tobgp, esoph)
remix(cbind(...) ~ Species, iris)
@
